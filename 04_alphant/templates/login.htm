Alphant is an experimental Mastodon client that shows you posts by the people you follow sorted alphabetically by peoples usernames.

privacy statement: I store the data I need to store to provide the service, and none besides. I will not look at your data except to fix things when they break. You can delete your data by pressing the "Revoke" button. This is a hobbyist project, service is best-effort. Source code available.
